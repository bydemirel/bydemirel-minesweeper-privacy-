# Privacy Policy

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how **Mine Sweeper** (also referred to as *Minefield*) collects, uses, and protects user information.

**Mine Sweeper** is a simple puzzle game designed for players of all ages, including children. The game is provided free of charge and is intended to be used as is.

By using this Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting user privacy, especially for children. Any information collected is used only to improve the game experience and is never sold or misused.

## Information Collection and Use
Mine Sweeper does **not require account creation** and does **not collect personally identifiable information** such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or social media data.

The game may collect **limited, non-personal, anonymous information** strictly for technical and performance purposes, such as:
- Device type and operating system version
- Anonymous crash and error reports
- Basic gameplay performance metrics

This data cannot be used to identify individual users and is only used to improve stability and gameplay quality.

## Third-Party Services
Mine Sweeper may use trusted third-party services solely to support game functionality, such as analytics or crash reporting tools.

These services are required to comply with applicable privacy laws and are not permitted to use collected data for advertising or profiling children.

## Cookies
Mine Sweeper does **not use cookies**.

Any third-party libraries used by the game may rely on similar technologies strictly for technical purposes. Users can manage such settings through their device preferences.

## Location Information
Mine Sweeper does **not collect location data**, including precise or approximate location information.

## Device Information
Anonymous device information may be collected to ensure compatibility, improve performance, and prevent technical issues. This information does not identify users and is safe for use in child-friendly applications.

## Service Providers
We may work with third-party service providers to:
- Operate and maintain the game
- Improve performance and reliability
- Analyze technical issues

These providers are granted limited access to data only as necessary and are contractually obligated to protect user privacy.

## Security
We take reasonable measures to protect information used by the game. However, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.

## Children’s Privacy
Protecting children’s privacy is especially important to us.

Mine Sweeper does **not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13**. The game does not include chat features, social interactions, or user-generated content.

If a parent or guardian believes that personal information has been unintentionally collected, they may contact us and we will promptly remove such data.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and will take effect immediately.
